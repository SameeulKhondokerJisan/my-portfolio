Heart Failure Prediction
Problem: Early, data-driven prediction of heart failure risk can enable timely clinical intervention and save lives.
Personal ML Project · Healthcare · EDA + SHAP Analysis
Chose Logistic Regression over higher-accuracy black-box models deliberately — in clinical settings, understanding WHICH features drive predictions matters as much as accuracy itself. Applied SHAP analysis across 12 clinical features to identify ejection fraction and serum creatinine as the two strongest risk predictors.
